How it works

Set it up once.
Forget about it forever.

Three steps, then OhSheet handles the rest on autopilot.

🔑

Connect Canvas

Grab your Canvas API token from your account settings and add it to OhSheet. That's it — read-only, no passwords, no OAuth dance.

⚡

OhSheet syncs automatically

OhSheet runs on a schedule and pulls your upcoming assignments from every course — deduplicating as it goes so you never get duplicates.

📊

Open your Sheet

Your Google Sheet stays fresh and organized — sorted by due date, color-coded by urgency, with checkboxes ready to strike through completed work.

Auto-sync on a schedule

OhSheet runs automatically on a cron schedule — no manual exports, no browser extensions, no remembering to run anything. Your sheet just stays current.
